About

Serving startups, small and medium size businesses since 2006. We have been helping people develop their business ideas. We programming, branding design and business development consulting. Our team is ready to help your business disrupt in your industry. We can team up with you in your venture or work as consultants.

Services

We can help you with your company's visual identity, graphic design, UI/UX design, concept development and programming. We also offer business consulting services to further cover your needs and help your company stand out from its competitors.

Process

We implemented a simple four-step process to address our case studies.

Stage 1: Meeting

In this stage we arrange a meeting aimed to achieve an understanding of the client's industry or business proposal.

Stage 2: Research

We will perform our research to elaborate the best action plan. We discuss our findings and insight with the client in order to proceed.

Stage 3: Development

In this stage we go to our creative laboratory to design concept, branding, UI/UX and application programming.

Stage 4: Delivery

We deliver the final product, go live, address questions, personnel trainings and troubleshooting.
